What is Roblox?

Discover the amazing world of Roblox and how it's changing the gaming landscape

Roblox is a global online platform that brings people together through play. It allows users to create, share, and play games and experiences with friends and millions of other people across various devices.

What makes Roblox unique is that everything in the platform is designed and built by members of the community. Roblox is designed for users of all ages, with a particular focus on teens and young adults who are interested in gaming, creation, and social interaction.

The platform combines elements of gaming, social media, and content creation, providing a space where users can not only play games but also create their own experiences using Roblox Studio.

Earn Robux

History of Roblox

From a small project to a global phenomenon

1989

The Beginning

Founder David Baszucki creates a physics simulation called "Interactive Physics" that would later inspire Roblox.

2004

Development Starts

Development of Roblox begins under the name "DynaBlocks". The founders, David Baszucki and Erik Cassel, work on creating a platform that combines physics, building, and scripting.

2005

Name Change

The name changes from "DynaBlocks" to "Roblox" - a portmanteau of "robots" and "blocks". The beta version of Roblox is launched.

2006

Official Launch

Roblox officially launches to the public. The platform starts gaining traction with its first games and users.

2021

Going Public

Roblox goes public with a direct listing on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker RBLX.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Roblox

What is Roblox and how does it work?

Roblox is an online platform and game creation system that allows users to design and play games created by other users. It works by providing a suite of tools through Roblox Studio where developers can create games using the Lua programming language. Players can then access these games through the Roblox website or mobile apps.

Is Roblox safe for children?

Roblox has several safety features in place, including chat filters, content moderation, and parental controls. Parents can restrict chat features, set spending limits, and monitor their child's activity. However, as with any online platform, it's important for parents to stay involved and educate their children about online safety.

How can I start creating games on Roblox?

To start creating games on Roblox, download Roblox Studio for free from the official website. There are many tutorials available on the Roblox Developer Hub and YouTube to help you learn the basics. Start with simple projects and gradually work your way up to more complex games as you gain experience.

What programming language does Roblox use?

Roblox primarily uses Lua, specifically a modified version called Lua, for scripting within games. Luau maintains compatibility with standard Lua while providing additional features and optimizations. For the platform itself, Roblox uses C++ for the core engine and various other languages for different components like the website and mobile apps.

How do I add friends on Roblox?

To add friends on Roblox, follow these steps: 1) Find the player you want to add as a friend in a game or on their profile page, 2) Click on their username or profile picture, 3) Select the "Add Friend" button on their profile, 4) Wait for them to accept your friend request. Once accepted, you'll be able to see when they're online, join their games, and chat with them.
